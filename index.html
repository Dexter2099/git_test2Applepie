Apple Pie Recipes

Apple Crumble Pie

  • Ingredients:
  • - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • - 2 tsp baking powder
  • - 1 tsp salt
  • - 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • - 1/2 cup white sugar
  • Unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • - 2 eggs
  • - 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • - 1/2 cup applesauce
  • - 1/4 cup milk
  • - 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  1. Preparation:
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, combine brown sugar and white sugar.
  5. Mix together the butter and eggs in a third bowl.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the applesauce and milk.
  7. Stir in the vanilla extract and nuts (if using).
  8. Spread the mixture into a 9" pie plate.
  9. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is set and the crust is golden brown.
  10. Let the pie cool for a few minutes, then turn it out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Traditional Apple Pie

  • Ingredients:
  • - 6 cups sliced apples
  • - 3/4 cup white sugar
  • - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • - 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 1 cup white sugar
  • - 1 egg
  • - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • - 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  1. Preparation:
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  3. Prepare the pastry and place it into a 9-inch pie pan.
  4. Combine 3/4 cup sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  5. Arrange the apples in the pastry shell.
  6. Cream the butter and 1 cup of sugar in a bowl.
  7. Add the egg and vanilla extract, mix well, and pour over the apples.
  8. Cover with the top crust and seal the edges. Cut small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
